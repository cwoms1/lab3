Spaghetti with Meatballs

Delicious Homemade Italian Classic

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/4 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 24 ounces of your favorite marinara sauce
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a large bowl, combine ground beef, ground pork, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, chopped parsley, egg, salt, black pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, oregano, basil, and red pepper flakes (if desired).
  2. Mix well until all ingredients are evenly incorporated.
  3. Form the mixture into meatballs of your desired size.
  4. In a large skillet, heat some olive oil over medium heat.
  5. Add the meatballs and cook until browned on all sides and cooked through, about 15 minutes.
  6. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  7. Heat the marinara sauce in a separate saucepan.
  8. Once the meatballs are cooked, add them to the marinara sauce and simmer for a few minutes.
  9. Serve the meatballs and sauce over cooked spaghetti.
  10. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.

This spaghetti with meatballs recipe is my absolute favorite because it's a classic Italian dish that combines savory meatballs with a flavorful marinara sauce. The blend of herbs and spices makes it incredibly delicious. Plus, it's easy to prepare and perfect for family dinners or special occasions.
